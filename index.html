About Manan

Hello! I'm Manan, a 14-year-old self-taught full-stack web developer from India. My journey into coding began with a fascination for how websites work, leading me to dive deep into HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a passion for building robust and beautiful web solutions.

I specialize in creating dynamic, user-friendly, and responsive websites for businesses of all sizes. My goal is to help clients establish a strong online presence and achieve their digital objectives. I'm also a big fan of Linux and enjoy exploring new technologies.

One of my notable client projects includes BusnTaxi.com, where I contributed to developing a seamless online platform. I'm always eager to learn, innovate, and take on new challenges.
